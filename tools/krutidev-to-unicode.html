About Krutidev Font

Krutidev 010 is one of the most widely used legacy Hindi fonts in India. It was extensively used in government offices, newspapers, and publishing houses before Unicode became the standard. Krutidev uses a non-standard encoding where Latin characters are mapped to Hindi glyphs, making the text unreadable without the Krutidev font installed.

This converter accurately transforms Krutidev encoded text into standard Unicode Devanagari, handling complex features like:

  • Half-letters and conjuncts (संयुक्त अक्षर)
  • Reph (र्) positioning
  • Chhoti ee matra (ि) reordering
  • Nukta characters (क़, ख़, ग़, etc.)
  • Special ligatures and half-forms
