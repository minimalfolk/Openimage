About Chanakya Font

Chanakya (also known as Walkman Chanakya) is a legacy Hindi font that was popular in the 1990s and early 2000s. It was commonly used in Hindi newspapers, magazines, and government publications. The AkrutiDev Chanakya variant is also widely found in older documents.

This converter accurately handles Chanakya's unique character mapping, including its distinctive treatment of vowel signs and conjunct consonants.
