About Shree Lipi Font

Shree Lipi (also known as ShreeDev) is a popular legacy font family used extensively in Maharashtra and other Hindi-speaking regions. ShreeDev 0714, 0708, and other variants were widely used in newspaper publishing, book typesetting, and government documentation.

This converter handles the unique encoding of Shree Lipi fonts including proper handling of matras, conjuncts, and the reph character positioning that differs from other legacy fonts.
