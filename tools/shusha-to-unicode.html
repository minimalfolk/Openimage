About Shusha Font

Shusha is a legacy Hindi font that was popular in the early days of Hindi computing. It uses a unique character encoding different from Krutidev and other fonts. Shusha was commonly used in academic publications, literary magazines, and some government departments.

This converter accurately maps Shusha's encoding to standard Unicode Devanagari, preserving all characters, matras, and conjuncts.
