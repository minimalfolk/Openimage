About Webdunia Font

Webdunia is a legacy Hindi font that was used on the Webdunia.com portal and related Hindi web services in the early 2000s before Unicode adoption became widespread. It uses an encoding similar to Krutidev, making conversion straightforward with our tool.

If you have old content from Webdunia-based systems, this converter helps you bring it into standard Unicode format for use on modern websites and applications.
